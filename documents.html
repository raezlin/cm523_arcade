About After Hours 24

Our mission is for people to have fun and enjoy the 80s vibe online. The website is an alternative way to play traditional arcade games online. Instead of a website that provides a list of game selections, we aim to create an environment that allows users to move around as a character and feel like they are in a game right after they enter the site by giving the website a design of an arcade room in pixel art.
